Transforming Disaster Relief in the Philippines

BayanihanAid is a centralized platform that connects barangays, donors, and government agencies to ensure transparent, efficient, and equitable distribution of relief goods during disasters.

Solving Critical Relief Challenges

Current disaster relief operations face systemic issues that prevent effective aid distribution

Resource Scarcity
No centralized view of barangay needs leads to uneven aid distribution
Supply Mismatch
No real-time matching between donors and actual needs
Poor Prioritization
Lack of priority logic results in first-come distribution
No Tracking
No per-household tracking enables double-dipping

Comprehensive Relief Management

Our platform provides end-to-end solutions for transparent and efficient disaster relief operations

Barangay Resource Requests
Centralized platform for barangays to submit detailed relief good requests with real-time status tracking.
Smart Resource Matching
AI-powered matching system connects available supplies with actual needs, preventing oversupply and shortages.
Distribution Analytics
Real-time monitoring and analytics to optimize resource allocation and identify critical gaps.
Transparent Operations
Complete transparency in relief operations with audit trails and public reporting to prevent corruption.
Household Tracking
Per-household tracking system ensures equitable distribution and prevents double-dipping.
Priority-Based Distribution
Intelligent prioritization based on urgency, vulnerability, and critical needs assessment.
